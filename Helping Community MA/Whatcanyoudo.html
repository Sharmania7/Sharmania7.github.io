    1. Donate

    Almost all non-profits provide an option for donating. Donating to environmental non-profits helps to support them as they work towards their mission. Non-profits may allow donations in the form of money, stocks, listing it as a beneficiary, and even donating used vehicles.

    2. Volunteering Oppurtunities

    Organization Name What it does How you can volunteer
    Sierra Club Forest Preservation through Trail maintenance, Clean Air and Water Participate in trail maintenance hikes, Data Entry/Administrative help, Graphic Design, Social Media
    World Wildlife Fund Plastic cleaning, Wildlife preservation, Solving Deforestation Adopt a rescued animal, March/Protest
    Greenpiece Halts nuclear testing and poaching of whales Participating in Action Pods, Spreading Awareness, Social media

    3. Fundraise

    Fundraising helps not only to raise money and materials for the cause, but organizing events like this can spread awareness and create a strong community for the cause.
